About Xiast

Xiast (Xiast is a scheduling tool) is a web- and android scheduling application designed and implemented by students of computer-science at the VUB (Vrije Universiteit Brussel).

This application provides users the ability to compose their personal schedule and notifies them about any change made by the university (VUB). Users also have the ability to search for unoccupied classrooms with specific equipment.

Xiast is accessible for everyone. People with no VUB-account can log in as guest and can compose and save their own personal schedule.
